Tableau Dashboard
This dashboard shows the relationship between many variables. These relationships can be filtered in various ways to gain a variety of insights.
Project Details
This dashboard can be filter by using the drop-down menus at the top of the dashboard or by selecting one or multiple points on the scatter plots. Additionally, the bar graphs can each be sorted to see the highest and lowest preforming city in each category and the bar graph can then also used as a filter to investigate further.
